ABOUT THIS PAGE


          Why?

            Back when I was applying for NYUAD, I wanted to get a closer look to the university's campus.
        
           To me however, the official website of NYUAD did not provide enough student's view or their life on campus.
        
           From such concerns, I have decided to create a website that shows the campus in the students' point of view.
        
           With the 'Walk With Me' video and the gallery section that shows various sites within the campus,
        
           I believe  this website will give insights to many people who wants to get to know NYUAD.


         About Myself
           I am a student from NYUAD, class of 2024. I'm really happy to get a chance to show other people,
        
           including my parents, a glimpse of how life at nyuad is from the student's point of view.
        
           I am always open for questions and others, so if interested to applying to our school or anything else,
